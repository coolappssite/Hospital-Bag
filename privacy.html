Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 January 2026

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No names, emails, or identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores checklist data locally on your device only, such as checked items and custom items you add. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Sharing

When you use the Share feature (text or PDF), content is shared only when you explicitly choose to share it using iOS system sharing tools. We do not receive or store any shared content.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising services, or tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect personal data and is safe to use without providing any information.
